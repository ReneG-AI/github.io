Welcome to Lavendish

Nestled in the heart of Lleida, Lavendish Bar offers a sophisticated retreat from the ordinary. Our carefully curated drink menu features artisanal cocktails crafted with precision and premium spirits selected from around the world.

Since opening our doors in 2018, we've created a space where quality beverages, warm hospitality, and thoughtful design come together to create memorable experiences.

Whether you're joining us for after-work drinks, celebrating a special occasion, or simply enjoying a night out, our experienced mixologists and attentive staff are dedicated to making your visit exceptional.
