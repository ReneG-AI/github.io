Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation?

Reservations are recommended, especially for weekends and special events, but not required. You can make a reservation by calling us at +34 123 456 789 or through our contact form.

Is there a dress code?

We have a smart casual dress code. While we don't require formal attire, we do ask that guests avoid sportswear, beachwear, and flip-flops in the evening.

Can I host a private event at Lavendish?

Yes, we offer private event services for birthdays, corporate events, and other special occasions. Our space can accommodate up to 80 guests. Please contact us with your requirements and we'll be happy to discuss options.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan tapas. These items are marked on our menu, and our staff is always happy to accommodate dietary restrictions whenever possible.

Is there parking available?

There is a public parking garage located one block from Lavendish Bar. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area, but can be limited during peak hours.
